Medium

Current senior sharing is limited and only occurs during the Academic day. We want to implement this on a more regular basis where such content can be accessed easily by all freshmen of the School of Computing. We want to establish a platform where seniors can share their experiences so freshmen can get a clearer idea of what to expect for their university years. Senior Sharing will focus on topics like first internship, orbital related matters, Student Exchange Programme, NUS Overseas College, module mapping as well as advice for how to build up your resume.
