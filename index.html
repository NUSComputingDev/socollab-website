SoCollab

SoCollab is a new initiative under the Student Development Wing of the 24th Management Committee which aspires to instil in students a passion for achieving excellence and holistic development.

It comprises three distinct components which will allow individuals to develop their interests as well as hone relevant skills sets required to be a leader in their chosen field.
