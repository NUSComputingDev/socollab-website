Medium is an open publishing platform where readers can share insightful stories on topics that interest them the most. As part of our cell’s objective to allow students from the School of Computing to discover their interests, we aim to start our own publication known as ‘SoCollab’. This publication will be responsible for publishing articles written by students from our faculty, pertaining to a multitude of computing disciplines like Data Science and Machine Learning among others, as well as practical experience, such as project work and internships.
