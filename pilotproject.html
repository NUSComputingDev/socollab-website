Medium

Our Pilot Projects initiative aims to connect like-minded students who are interested in taking on interesting group projects but face difficulties in finding members to complete them with.

These projects will give students the opportunity to build up the technical skills in the realm they are interested in, as well as expand their network by connecting with fellow students from the School of Computing. These projects are also an opportunity for students to write an article for the SoCollab Medium page, and we will highly encourage students to do so when they complete their projects.
